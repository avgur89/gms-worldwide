Mobile and location-based advertising

Mobile advertising is a more targeted way of advertising. Based on mobile subscribers data analysis and profiling, information about your products or services is delivered to your customers. Location-based ads allow you to reach your customer right where and when they want to be reached, thus increasing chances of conversion. While people can miss out on a generic email targeted SMS messages are opened instantly, so a timely automated, targeted offer based on the users location significantly increase the probabilities of bringing you a new customer right on the spot.
